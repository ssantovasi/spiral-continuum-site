# The Resurfacing *(working title)*

## Capstone

*The secret was not hidden away. It was hidden forward.*

The closing volume of *Island of Bones*. Currently in beat-sheet
form.

The fragment of the Codec that the Guardians sealed at the end of
Book Three — the piece that was *not for now* — keys to a condition
the behiques could not name when they cut it. When the condition is
finally met, the Codec opens its last door.

Book Nine is the answer to a question Book One taught its readers
to ask without ever asking it directly. The pale man, alive across
a thousand years, closes the loop he opened in ~1000 AD when he
walked off the Vinland window and turned south.

The saga turns from "they found the secret" into "the secret was
*waiting* for them."

The work the behiques did across five hundred years — to hide truth
forward — pays off on a scale only the final volume can show.
